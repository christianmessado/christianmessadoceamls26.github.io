what_i_learned: |
  Today marked the beginning of our final week of the CEAMLS Summer Research Internship. With the Final Symposium approaching, our main focus shifted toward preparing and refining our final deliverables. Our team worked on improving our presentation slides, reviewing our speaking points, and making sure our research was clearly communicated. We also spent time reviewing our Overleaf research paper to ensure that the content, formatting, and overall structure were polished and ready for submission.

  I learned that presenting research requires a different level of preparation compared to conducting the research itself. After spending weeks building our pipeline, training models, and analyzing results, this stage is about effectively communicating our work and showing the impact of our project.

With the symposium coming up, we needed to balance improving our presentation, reviewing the research paper, and planning out the remaining work for the week. Making sure everything was consistent and aligned required strong teamwork and communication.
