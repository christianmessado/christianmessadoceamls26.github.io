what_i_learned: |
  Today was a refreshing change of pace as the program organized a field day for everyone after seven weeks of research, presentations, coding, and collaboration. Since we had officially completed the research portion of the internship, the program directors wanted to reward us with a day to relax, have fun, and decompress before the Final Symposium.

  Throughout the day, we participated in a variety of games and activities that encouraged teamwork, friendly competition, and collaboration. The teams were mixed across different research groups, so I wasn't paired with my usual teammates. Although it was different from what I expected, it gave me the opportunity to meet and bond with other participants in the program whom I hadn't worked closely with before.

  Today reminded me that experiences like this internship are about more than just research. Building friendships, creating memories, and connecting with people from different backgrounds are just as valuable as the technical skills we've gained over the past seven weeks.

blockers: |
  There were no major challenges today since the focus was on relaxing and enjoying time together after completing our research. The only "challenge" was adjusting to working with a completely different team during the activities, but it ended up being a great opportunity to get to know other members of the program.

reflection: |
  Today was one of the most enjoyable days of the internship. After weeks of deadlines, coding, writing papers, and preparing presentations, it felt rewarding to take a step back and celebrate everything we had accomplished together. The field day was a great reminder of how much our cohort has grown—not only as researchers, but also as a community.

  Although I wasn't on the same team as my research group, I really enjoyed getting to know other participants and building new friendships. As the program comes to a close, I'm grateful for all of the experiences, lessons, and memories I've made throughout these seven weeks. It was the perfect way to recharge before the Final Symposium.
