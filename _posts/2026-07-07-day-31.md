what_i_learned: |
  Today was primarily focused on preparing for our upcoming research poster presentation by searching for a professional and effective poster template. Before adding any content, we wanted to find a layout that would clearly communicate our research while maintaining a clean and visually appealing design. We explored several poster formats and evaluated how different layouts organized sections such as the introduction, objectives, methodology, literature review, results, and conclusions.

  Throughout the process, I learned that choosing the right template is an important first step in creating a successful research poster. A well-designed layout helps guide the audience through the research story and makes complex information easier to understand. We also discussed how visual elements, such as diagrams, flowcharts, and figures, can make technical concepts more accessible and engaging during presentations.

  By the end of the day, we had a better understanding of how we wanted to structure our poster and were ready to begin filling in each section with the work we have completed throughout the internship.

blockers: |
  One challenge was finding a template that balanced professionalism with enough flexibility to showcase our research effectively. Many templates either contained too much unused space or did not organize the sections in a way that fit our project. We spent time comparing different designs before deciding on one that would best support our presentation.

reflection: |
  Although today's progress was more focused on planning than technical development, it was an important milestone in preparing for the symposium. Investing time in selecting the right poster template will make it much easier to organize our research and present it clearly. As we move forward, we can now focus on adding our methodology, workflow, literature review, and results into a polished presentation that effectively highlights the work our team has accomplished.
