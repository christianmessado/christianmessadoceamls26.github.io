what_i_learned: |
  Today our team continued working on our elevator pitch video as the deadline was quickly approaching. Since we decided to use a news report theme for our video, we focused on making the presentation feel more creative and engaging while still effectively explaining our research project.

  We traveled to multiple locations to capture different scenes and footage for the video. Being able to film in different     areas helped us create a more realistic news-style presentation and made the elevator pitch feel more like a professional    segment rather than a traditional presentation. I also worked on editing the videos together, organizing the clips, and      making sure the transitions and overall flow matched the theme we were going for.

  This experience taught me that presenting research can involve creativity beyond just slides and technical explanations.     Finding unique ways to communicate our work can make the message more memorable and help connect with a wider audience.

Since we were creating a news report-style video, there were many different elements to        coordinate, including the locations, footage, and overall storytelling.

  Another challenge was making sure the technical information about our project was still clearly explained while              maintaining the creative style of the video.
